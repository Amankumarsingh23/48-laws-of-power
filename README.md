# The 48 Laws of Power

This repository contains a comprehensive breakdown of Robert Greene's "The 48 Laws of Power" - a bestselling book exploring the dynamics of power in human interactions and social hierarchies.

## Overview

The 48 Laws of Power is a book that outlines principles of gaining and maintaining power in professional and personal relationships. Each law is presented with explanations, examples, and practical applications.
